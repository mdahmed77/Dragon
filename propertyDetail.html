Description

Welcome home to your perfect desert retreat located in the much-coveted central Palm Springs Sunrise Park neighborhood conveniently close to downtown restaurants, shopping and Palm Springs International Airport. This Ranch style 3 bedroom, plus office/den, and 2 bath home basks in natural light with an open and airy vaulted ceiling in the living room, fireplace, large kitchen with newer cabinetry, appliances, granite counters, and dining room that leads to ample seating and dining areas under the covered patio, complete with ceiling fans, professional misting system and an outdoor shower. The spacious backyard includes a salt water pool, refinished pool deck and tile. The drought tolerant desert landscaping includes orange and tangelo trees, a pomegranate bush and a cabana positioned to take advantage of the mountain views. There is a separate laundry room and oversized 2 car attached garage with extra storage. Other renovations and updates completed over the last few years include newer terra cotta roof, leased solar panels, newer dual zone AC and furnace, ceramic tile flooring throughout. Fresh interior and exterior paint and renovated bathrooms make this home move-in-ready. Enjoy the Palm Springs lifestyle on land that you own!
